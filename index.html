Welcome to CBC Lesson Planner 🏘

Plan your Grade 1–9 lessons for the week in just a few steps. Here’s how it works:

1
Pick your grade
At the top of the page, click your class grade (Gr 1 through Gr 9). The subject list and competencies will update automatically for that grade level.
🇰🇪
Gr 1  Gr 4  Gr 7
Green = currently selected grade
2
Open a subject accordion
In the left panel, click any subject name (e.g. Mathematics) to expand it and reveal all competency cards for that subject. Click it again to collapse and save space.
Mathematics 8 ▲
↓ Competency cards appear here
3
Drag a competency onto a day
Each card has a drag grip (⋮⋮) on the left. Click and hold any card, then drag it onto one of the five day columns (Monday – Friday) on the right. Drop it and it’s planned!
📋
MTH.4.1.a
Divide numbers up to 1000…
→ drag to Tuesday column
4
Navigate weeks
Use Prev and Next buttons to plan ahead or look back. Each week is saved separately. Your plans are never lost when switching grades or weeks.
5
Export or print your plan
Click Print / PDF to open a clean, printable version of your weekly plan. Use your browser’s Save as PDF option to keep a digital copy.
6
Add your own competencies
Click + Add custom competency at the bottom of the left panel to add your own learning outcomes. They will appear in the library alongside the built-in ones.
💡 Quick tip Use the search bar at the top of the left panel to quickly find any competency by keyword or code. Type “fraction” or “MTH.5” to filter instantly.
