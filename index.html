How it works — 4 simple steps
1
Pick your grade & subject
Use the Grade buttons in the top bar to switch between Grades 1–9. The sidebar updates automatically with the correct CBC subjects and competencies for that level (Lower, Upper or Junior Secondary).
🎓
2
Drag competencies onto the week
Click a subject tab in the sidebar to see its learning outcomes. Drag any card from the sidebar and drop it onto a day column in the weekly planner. Each card shows the competency code and outcome description.
🖱️
3
Navigate weeks & add custom competencies
Use Prev / Next to move between weeks — each grade and week saves separately. Use the Add competency form at the bottom of the sidebar to create your own learning outcomes and add them to any subject.
📅
4
Export or print your plan
Click Print / PDF to generate a clean, print-ready lesson plan table for the week. Your plans save automatically in this browser — no account required.
📄
💡 Pro tip: Use the Subject coverage panel at the bottom of the planner to check you're distributing lessons evenly across subjects each week.
